# Explanation

Conceptual material on the metrics, the `GraphLike` Protocol, and
the design choices that distinguish `bnm` v0.2 from its v0.1.x
predecessor. These pages are aimed at readers who want to
understand *why* the package is structured the way it is — or who
plan to cite the library in a paper.
